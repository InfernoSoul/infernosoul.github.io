Description

Underworld Dungeon is a rogue-like action-adventure releasing in late July, in which the player explores a ruined Greek dungeon hidden within the depths of the Underworld. As they dig deeper, ancient weapons and armor will be uncovered which can be combined to create more powerful gear. The player will need all the items that they can scavenge and craft to conquer the challenging dungeon; a dungeon that masks unspeakable horrors, mythical secrets and finally, the promise of a 'priceless treasure', which awaits them at the end of it all.

Inspired by games such as The Binding of Isaac, Dark Souls and Terraria, Underworld Dungeon aims to create a dark, procedurally generated world filled with secrets and items to discover as well as a challenging combat system which will require the player to use all their wits and skills to confront each enemy and situation.

Inspiration

Underworld Dungeon takes great inspiration from 'The Legend of Zelda' as well as fellow roguelikes such as 'The Binding of Isaac' and 'Nuclear Throne'. The game also takes it's crafting, gear and inventory system from one seen in Terraria, as well as taking the combat style seen in Dark Souls as a basis for that in the game.

Featurelist

  • Over 150 weapons, armour pieces and gear
  • A semi-permament death system, allowing for new items to be unlocked for future playthroughs
  • A fully fledged crafting system, allowing for the most devastating of weapons
  • Full Steamworks support, with Achievements and Leaderboards included
  • Many bosses and enemies for the player to encounter during runs
  • Many secrets and NPC's to be discovered inside the dungeon
  • Continual support for the game for at least 12 months post-release

Release

Although we initially plan on releasing only through Steam, we also eventually plan on adding the game through services such as GOG and the Humble Store. The game will retail for $9.99, but will also provide a deluxe edition for $14.99, including the OST, a Digital Art Book and a Digital Guide for the game.
